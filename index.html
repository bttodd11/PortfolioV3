5 Foundations

Style

Style is a foundation that I base every UI/UX on. When looking to make a site you should want something unique that not a lot of people have seen. To receive this the collaboration of your ideas and my skills will be seen in the interface.

Responsiveness

Before deploying a UI I run through making sure that it will look good on all devices. Everything from cell phones to tablets and monitors your website will look good on each device it is viewed on.

Delivery

Every UI will be shown on what went into the design and also what functions the interface has. I will present that the site to you and not just hand it off. At this moment if anything altered then these changes will be made and be represented to your until everything meets your needs.

Dedication

Every UI that I create I will be proud of, if it is not made up to specs then I will work on it until it is. Time does not matter, if it takes a little longer then expected then I will work on it until it is finished with quality. My purpose is not to push out a bunch of interfaces but instead, create quality interfaces.

Availability

If any changes need to be made or anything needs to be updated I will be easily reachable. The length of updating a UI will be because of the time spent working on it not because I am too busy. My response will be ASAP and getting a hold of me is going to be convenient throughout our time working together.
